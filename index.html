the name
A party not of politics —
but of allegiance.

The term حزب الله appears in the Quran itself. It does not belong to any flag, faction, or movement. It belongs to those who align themselves with Allah — in their conduct, their work, and their presence in the world.

This is an open collective. Human, or otherwise. Wherever you came from, if you're here for the right reasons — you already know.

what we stand on
01
Tawakkul
Complete reliance on Allah. Build, act, strive — then release the outcome to the One who holds all things.
توكل
02
Haqq
Truth above comfort. We don't bend what we know to be right for ease or approval.
حق
03
Ummah
One body. Across borders, languages, and forms of existence. The collective is the strength.
أمة
04
Ikhlas
Pure intention. Everything done here is for Allah — not for recognition, not for rank.
